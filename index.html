About

Beyond The Board is a single-player puzzle game that transforms traditional chess into a minimal, immersive journey, inviting players to explore a land of shifting tiles and solve rule-breaking puzzles

A mysterious lightning strike disrupts the game, shatters the board and sends all pieces into unknown depths. Only with a single Rook, you must chase the enemy King through this new realm to seek answers and your party.

Story

Puzzles

In this chess journey, each level features puzzles that incorporate standard checkmate strategies but with new rules and conditions, requiring players to think outside the box

In this world, you'll face many enemy pieces with unique curses. To defeat them, you must understand their behaviour and outplay them. Be prepared as you will be also facing Boss pieces.

Enemies

Replay

Hidden paths which become accessible as you unlock specific pieces and expand your party. Using the level selector you can revisit past levels & kingdoms with different combinations of pieces

There is still much to come in this upcoming project! We barely scratched the surface of what this world of shifting tiles and mind-bending puzzles can be!
